About Delhi

Let the glimpse of history touch your soul, the political aura make you powerful and the winds of north tingle your spine. The numerous places to visit in Delhi will not only captivate you, but will also leave you astounded with its vibrant culture. The capital city of India is blessed with an array of historical sites, beautiful temples and architectural ruins. The neighbouring hill stations and cities that Delhi hosts make awesome getaway for all the adventurers. The city has an intriguing number of historical resorts to visit so experience the rich heritage by taking a tour to the Red Fort, to Qutb Minar and India Gate and several other monuments. Visit the pristine Lotus Temple, the Jama Masjid , the Iskcon temple and many other places of worship, to feel the divinity they proffer.
