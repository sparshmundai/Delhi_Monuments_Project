Humayun’s Tomb

Humayun' s Tomb

Precursor to Taj Mahal, Mughal Architecture, A Tomb, Many Water Channels, A Pavilion and A Bath. This World Heritage Sites in India was built by Begum Bega, the first wife of Humayun between 1565-1572. It is the only monument which has gone several restoration work and is complete. It's a tomb complex consisting of several smaller monuments including the tombs of Isa Khan Niyazi, an Afghan noble in Sher Shah Suri's court of the Suri dynasty.
